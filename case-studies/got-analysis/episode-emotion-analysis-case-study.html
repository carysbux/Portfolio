Data visualization NLP & regression Web dashboard 2026

Introduction

This dashboard explores how emotional tone in episode synopses relates to success metrics. Emotion scores are extracted from text and modeled against ratings and audience data.

Goals

  • Identify which emotions correlate with higher ratings across review platforms.
  • Measure how emotional tone relates to U.S. viewership and runtime.
  • Visualize coefficient importance by target variable.
